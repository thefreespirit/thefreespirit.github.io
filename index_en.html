The free spirit alias Ines Ben El Hadj is a 20-year-old artist, born December 17th, 2000, in Casablanca, Morocco. L'

infini des cœurs

Summary :

It is a french poetic representation of the path of every human being towards self-love and self-acceptance; a path which is nevertheless full of difficulties given the limiting obstacles that sometimes are put by our own person, society and its judgments or it’s simply the various situations of life that limit us. However,this path leads to the most beautiful and precious treasure, for the existence of each of us .

And you, during your life, have you ever experienced a separation that was emotionally difficult for you? How did you experience it?

To fight poverty

There are different ways to fight poverty on a daily basis, and over time we have developed increasingly creative solutions to help fight poverty all over the world; here are some applications that allow you to contribute easily:

Thanks to Sharethemeal, you can feed a hungry child per day for just 0.70 Euros, and you can also track where meals are distributed and their impact:

Sharethemeal


Thanks to Charitymiles, For every kilometer you run, walk or cycle, an amount is donated to the organization of your choice by the current application sponsors. The donation might seem rather small - 10 cents / mile for people who use a bicycle and 25 cents / mile for runners and walkers - but seeing other people participating and increasing the amount will make you feel good and encourage you to follow along. your fitness program:

Charitymiles

Combattre la pauvreté (suite 1 )

This is a "free giving app," which means you don't have to send real money to a charity. Instead, the app asks you to donate one photo per day, which you can do occasionally or as often as every day. Each photo comes with a donation of $ 1 - so you can donate $ 365 per year:

Donateaphoto


This app is based on the idea that turns your time into money. It calculates the time you spend on your phone each day and converts it into charitable donations. So, 1500 points are awarded $ 2, while 3000 points earn $ 5, all of which are donated to a charity of your choice - you choose one from a list of participating charities.

Give2charity

Combattre la pauvreté (suite 2 )

This charity app rounds up your credit and debit card transactions and donates your spare change to the chosen charity. With this app, the change you forgot will be put to good use. Coin Up allows you to set a monthly goal or limit. You also have the choice of making a one-time donation or a fixed monthly donation. You also receive a donation receipt at the end of each year:

Coinapp


Freerice is another premier charity app from the World Food Program. Just play trivia games on the app, and the sponsors will take care of the donations; which means you won't be paying straight out of your pocket. In other words, not only will you be able to help with this app, but you will also learn something!
